Grow Business

Be seen how? Smart devices and social media.

Smart phones and tablets now drive about 48% of all online traffic and account for about 28% of all online sales. Your business WILL GROW by having a smart phone and tablet ready web site. Teaming up with a designer with business building skills in social media like Twitter, Pinterest, YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook and SEO skills with Google is absolutely necessary to have a pulse on the trends of the vast online marketplace and ‘big data’.

Ways I create business friendly environments include:
» Responsive design coding for smart devices
» Store building with platforms like Big Cartel, Word Press and others
» Beautiful UI design, illustration and branding
» Search engine optimization (SEO) with Google
» Traffic tracking and analysis with Google Analytics
» Social media networking
» Startup company quick prototyping, UI design and branding

** I also do mobile app and startups trend research and s​cout general opportunities within the mobile app and startups market,​ particularly in Silicon Valley, so if you are looking for partners, have ideas, etc., let’s talk!

Business case study

Client : Optimal Health Network, Madison WI, 2011-2014

Task : Madison WI entrepreneur and health guru Kristina Amelong needed her business sites to recover from lost Google rankings (and therefore lost revenue) due to an updated Google algorithm (Panda).

Result : We worked on multiple approaches to improve Google rankings: social media cultivation via Twitter, Facebook, YouTube and Pinterest (including a responsive, smart device friendly image gallery to pin images from to Pinterest), blog writing cultivation and extensive use of Google Analytics to track traffic. Results include page #1 Google rankings for YouTube and page #1-2 Google rankings for both web sites. Revenue was restored back to normal monthly values.
