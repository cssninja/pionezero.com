About Our Custom Corporate Bars

Yes, You Can Give a Pen...

Make a lingering impression with your customers and clients using Chocolate Chisel's customized chocolate bars and truffles.

Your marketing dollars are very precious and we know you must make the best and most lasting impression possible with what you have.

Yes, you can give a pen, a mug, or a pad of paper to reach your customers, but the truth is those items regularly get lost in the mug holding all of those pens and pretty soon your impression becomes blurred with everyone else's.

Not true with our chocolate and truffles! Our chocolate consistently leaves a lingering memory on one's palate and mind.

The Chocolate Chisel is known for its artisan designs and flavors, all crafted right here in our shop, nestled in the heart of Port Washington, Wisconsin, just 30 minutes north of downtown Milwaukee.

We are able to take your company logo and hand craft an artisan chocolate bar representing your unique style and 'flavor'. Bars can be custom-sized to make the strongest impact you are trying to achieve. To further impress, you can customize not only the size but also the ingredients that go into the chocolate. For example you can add fruit, nuts, caramels, herbs, spices-- any flavor profile you desire! We can design custom truffles to match your company colors. All of our truffles are skillfully painted by hand under the watchful eye of master chocolatier John Reichert.

Customized truffle boxes range in size from one to fifty pieces with a wide variety of tastes, colors and designs. We can customize gift baskets based on your needs and price point. It all starts with a call! Please contact Jennifer Golemba Vice President of Marketing and Sales, at (262) 261-0200 (store) to get started today!
